Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find tutors? ▼
Use the Find a tutor page, filter by subject, level, mode and location.
Is the platform free? ▼
Yes, browsing and signing up are free for both students and tutors.
How are lessons scheduled? ▼
Students and tutors coordinate directly based on availability and preferred mode.
Do you support Hybrid lessons? ▼
Yes, many tutors offer Online, Offline, and Hybrid modes.
What subjects are available? ▼
We offer Math, Physics, English, Computer Science, Chemistry, Biology, and Nepali.
How do I become a tutor? ▼
Sign up as a tutor, complete your profile, upload a photo, and pass the subject quiz.
What is the tutor quiz? ▼
A 10-question test in your chosen subject. You need 6/10 correct to pass.
How do I contact a tutor? ▼
Click "Show More" on any tutor profile (requires login) to see contact details.
Can I change my profile later? ▼
Yes, you can update your profile information anytime from your dashboard.
What if I fail the tutor quiz? ▼
You can retry after 1 month. Use this time to study and improve your knowledge.
Is there a mobile app? ▼
Currently we're web-based only, but our site is fully responsive for mobile devices.
